The Best Movies According to Shweta

My top 3 Movies of all time

Kabhi Khushi Kabhi gum

It's a family movie.And it's excellent.
"Zindagi mein agar kuch banna ho, kuch haasil karna ho, kuch jeetna ho ... toh hamesha dil ki suno ... aur agar dil bhi koi jawab na de toh aankhen band karke apni maa aur papa ka naam lo ... phir dekhna har manzil paar kar jaoge, har mushkil aasaan ho jayegi ... jeet tumhari hogi, sirf tumhari"Is one of the famous dialogue from the movie.

Yeh Jawani Hai Dewaani

It's fascinating and fun loving movie.
"Life mein jitna bhi try karo, kuch na kuch toh chootega hi.
Isliye jaha hai wahi ka mazza lete hai"Is one of the famous dialogue from the movie.

3 Idiots

This movie suggests that success should be defined by one's personal goals and happiness.
"Success Ke Peeche Mat Bhaago, Excellence Ka Peecha Karo, Success Jhak Maarke Tumhare Peeche Ayegi"Is one of the famous dialogue from the movie.
